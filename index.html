Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is kombucha?

Kombucha is a fermented tea drink made by adding specific strains of bacteria, yeast, and sugar to black or green tea. The fermentation process creates probiotics and other beneficial compounds that support gut health and overall wellness.

What makes Fizzora different from other kombuchas?

Fizzora stands out with its authentic Indian-inspired flavors and our proprietary "Flavor Lock" technique that preserves volatile spice aromas. We also focus on low/zero sugar formulations while maintaining great taste, and we're committed to sustainable, local production.

How much sugar is in Fizzora?

Unlike many other beverages, Fizzora is formulated to be low in sugar. Our products contain minimal natural sugars, with most varieties having less than 2g per 100ml. We never add artificial sweeteners, preservatives, or flavorings.

How should I store Fizzora and how long does it last?

Fizzora should be stored refrigerated for optimal taste and probiotic preservation. An unopened bottle will stay fresh for up to 3 months. After opening, consume within 3-5 days for the best flavor and benefits.

Is Fizzora suitable for vegans and people with food allergies?

Yes! Fizzora is 100% plant-based and vegan-friendly. Our products are free from common allergens including gluten, dairy, nuts, and soy. However, we always recommend checking the label if you have specific dietary concerns.
